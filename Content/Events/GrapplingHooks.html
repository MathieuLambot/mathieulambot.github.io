Grappling Hooks Event

Date~Late 2025
StatusConcluded
Tiers5

How It Worked

Crafting Tiers
Players could craft 5 tiers of Grapple Hooks using a special recipe — fishing rod in the center slot, surrounded by ore blocks. Higher ore tier = higher hook tier, with better velocity, range, speed, and lower cooldown.

How to use: Right-click to launch yourself. Useful for players without fly or hacks.

After the event ended, all crafting recipes were permanently disabled. Existing hooks became rare uncraftable items.
