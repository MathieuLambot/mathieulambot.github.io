What Happened

Phase 1 — The Theft
bluestöne placed 3 Gumball Machines at spawn. Before any announcement, elonMuschio stole them using a flying machine — but was robbed while AFK. RoxanDev (Discord: bigdaddy1221) then moved them even further away.
Phase 2 — Giveaway
bluestöne made the machines pickable via shift-click and added more around spawn. He also ran an official giveaway. Winner: idkwhat_to_put.
Phase 3 — The Duplication Glitch
elonMuschio discovered a duplication glitch using pistons, slabs, honey blocks, and trapdoors. He traded the method to bluestöne in exchange for one machine of every color, then continued duping massively. The glitch was never patched. He later traded duped machines with Losho16 (Discord: Pala) for Density X, Breach X, Lunge X, and Unbreaking X books — obtained during the concurrent Limitless Enchants Event.
