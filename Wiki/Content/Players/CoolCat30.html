Biography

CoolCat30 is a close friend of bluestöne and was present before the server's official launch. He was invited to test the survival prototype with custom crafting recipes — and despite the intentionally difficult design, still managed to kill the Ender Dragon during the preview session.

After bluestöne reset the world, CoolCat30 joined the official launch and teamed up with ThatFrost_ to win the Week 1 Event. The two then gambled their prize on a Double or Nothing bet.

His Hoglin Farm and Warden Farm were later raided and destroyed by VIP player StNDt.
