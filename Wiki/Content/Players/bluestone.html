Biography

bluestöne founded AnarchySMP in August 2025. Originally planned as a survival server with intentionally difficult custom crafting recipes, the project pivoted to Anarchy after difficulties finding a suitable spawn and completing the survival concept.

He has organized all official events and handles server infrastructure, which runs on a dedicated machine costing $200/month.
